Let's Explore New York City!

Welcome to the great city of New York! Named for the Duke of York who would later become King James II of England.
Located near the southern tip of New York State it houses one of the world's largest natural habours. It's also a global center of finance, technology, entertainment, and more.
As of 2023 the average median household income in New York was $79,713 which is generally higher than the rest of the state.
